Privacy Policy

FuelFit

Last Updated: June 19, 2026

1. Overview

FuelFit is a nutrition tracking and diet coaching application. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use our app. We believe your health data is yours alone.

2. No Accounts, No Tracking

FuelFit does not require you to create an account. We do not collect:

  • Your name, email, or any personal identifiers
  • Usage analytics or behavior tracking
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Location data

3. Data Stored Locally

All data you create in FuelFit is stored exclusively on your device using localStorage. This includes:

  • Meal entries and nutrition logs
  • Water intake records
  • Personal settings (weight, goals, dietary preferences)
  • AI provider API keys (encrypted)

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export or share it.

4. AI Photo Analysis

FuelFit offers AI-powered food photo analysis. When you use this feature:

  • Your photo is sent directly to the AI provider you select (e.g., Gemini, OpenAI, MiMo, Qwen)
  • You must provide your own API key for the chosen provider
  • Your API key is encrypted with an "enc:" prefix and stored locally on your device
  • We do not intermediate, log, or store any images or AI responses

Please refer to the privacy policy of your chosen AI provider for how they handle images and data.

5. No Third-Party Analytics

We do not integrate any third-party analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs. The app contains no trackers.

6. Data Security

Your data is stored using the device's native storage mechanisms. We recommend enabling device encryption (passcode/biometric lock) for additional protection. Since we don't have servers, there is no cloud data breach risk from our side.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data persists only on your device. You can delete all data at any time by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app. Uninstalling FuelFit permanently removes all locally stored data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date. We will not send notifications, as the app has no account system.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: samuel.hu [at] msn.com

By using FuelFit, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
