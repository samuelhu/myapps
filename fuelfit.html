Features

What FuelFit Does

Every feature is built around the core principle: powerful nutrition tools that respect your privacy.

📸

AI Food Photo Analysis

Snap a picture of your plate to instantly estimate calories, macros (protein, carbs, fat, fiber), sugar, sodium, and hydration. Supports Qwen, OpenAI, Mistral, and any OpenAI-compatible endpoint via Custom provider.

📊

Scientific Nutrition Scoring

View your daily FuelScore (0–100) calculated from calorie adherence, macro balance, micronutrient coverage, meal regularity, and satiety quality. Calories are recalculated from macros using scientific Atwater factors.

🧠

Personal AI Diet Coach

Get personalized feedback, actionable suggestions, and habit-aware tips tailored to your goals (maintain, lose, or gain weight). Toggle it off anytime for template-based coaching that runs entirely on your device.

📈

Insights & Patterns

Comprehensive nutrition scoring, streak tracking, weekly digest summaries, and visual trend analysis with beautiful charts. The "AI" badge appears only when coaching is genuinely generated by your provider.

🔐

True Privacy-First Design

No accounts, no servers, no tracking. All daily logs stored locally in IndexedDB. Your API keys are stored only on your device using built-in secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore) and never in IndexedDB or plain text. Keys are sent directly to your chosen provider over HTTPS — we never see or store them.

🌍

7 Languages + iOS Widgets

Fully localized in English, Simplified Chinese, German, Spanish, French, Japanese, and Korean. Sync your daily nutrition rings and hydration data to native iOS widgets. Themes include light, dark, and vibrant "Pulse" mode.

Bring Your Own API Key. FuelFit has no subscription fee. You choose the AI provider and pay them directly. Typical cost for an active user: ~$0.69/year with Qwen, ~$1.45/year with OpenAI. View the full API Provider Setup Guide →

AI Providers

Choose Your Provider

You bring your own key. FuelFit connects directly from your device. No intermediaries, no markups.

Cheapest · Default

Qwen / Alibaba Cloud

~$0.69/year
qwen3-vl-flash · Strong food recognition · Free credits for new accounts

New Alibaba Cloud accounts receive 1 million free tokens — enough for months of normal use. The qwen3-vl-flash model is specifically designed for image understanding and handles Chinese cuisine especially well. You can upgrade to qwen3-vl-plus for higher accuracy anytime.

Most Reliable

OpenAI

~$1.45/year
gpt-4o-mini · Predictable behavior · Broad ecosystem

Strong reliability and broad ecosystem support. Slightly more expensive than Qwen but extremely predictable. Pre-filled Base URL and model in FuelFit — swap to gpt-4o for higher accuracy if needed. Start with as little as $5.

EU-Based

Mistral

~$1.60/year
mistral-small-latest · Open-weights · EU jurisdiction

European AI company with open-weights models. Choose this for EU-jurisdiction processing or if you prefer Mistral's stack. Also offers a free tier with limited usage.

Power User

Custom Provider

Varies
Any OpenAI-compatible endpoint · OpenRouter · Self-hosted

Use Anthropic Claude via OpenRouter, Google Gemini, a self-hosted vLLM server, or any other OpenAI-compatible API. FuelFit needs a multimodal model that accepts images. The Base URL and Model fields are fully editable for complete flexibility.

Honest AI Label. When coaching falls back to built-in templates (toggle off, no key, or AI failure), the "AI" badge disappears from the Insights card so you always know what's actually generated. Read the full transparency guide →
