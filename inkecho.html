Features

What Makes It Special

Every feature is designed with children's cognitive development and learning science in mind.

🧠

Adaptive Memory Engine

Powered by an SM-2 spaced repetition algorithm tuned for children. Reviews are scheduled at just the right moment to maximize retention without causing fatigue.

📖

Original Illustrated Stories

Characters are introduced through beautifully illustrated storybooks that provide meaningful context. Children learn words by seeing them in action, not in isolation.

✍️

Handwriting Stroke Practice

Interactive stroke-order writing lets children practice proper character formation. Visual feedback and guided traces build muscle memory naturally.

🎯

Three-Tier Progression

From basic radicals to advanced compound characters, the curriculum follows a carefully designed progression that matches children's developmental stages.

📊

Parent Dashboard

Track learning progress, daily streaks, and mastered characters. Insights help parents understand their child's learning journey at a glance.

🔒

Privacy by Design

All learning data is stored locally on your device. No accounts, no servers, no data collection. Your child's learning stays private.
